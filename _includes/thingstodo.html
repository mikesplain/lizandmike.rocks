Things to do!

For Art Lovers

Founded in 1919, Munson-Williams has been the destination for countless school field trips! It houses an impressive collection of artwork and the school is associated with the Pratt school of art in N.Y.C. Also be sure to check out Fountain Elms which was the private residence of the founding family and is located on the same property.

Munson-Williams

For Animal Lovers

Visit the Utica Zoo! It’s family fun for everyone, at the Utica Zoo!
(Also- as a point of interest… the Utica Zoo is home to the World’s Largest Watering Can!! Be sure to check it out and snap a photo before you leave.)

Utica Zoo

For Beer Lovers

There’s multiple places to visit in the Utica area! Head to Woodland Hop Farm and Fermentation if you want a from the roots Micorbrewery/Beer Garden feel. (Its BYOF!)

Woodland Hop Farm and Fermentation

If you have time to schedule a tour- Saranac’s your spot. They sport a quality beer list in a historic building in the heart of downtown Utica. They are the second oldest family-owned brewery in the U.S. after Yeungling. So while we can’t best the Philadelphia crowd we’re a close second!

Saranac Brewery

For Spirits Lovers

We’ve never actually visited this spot but we've heard great things about Adirondack Distilling! It would be a nice change of pace from beer scene. Be sure to make a reservation if you’d like a tour!

Adirondack Distilling
